About Bhole bikewala Motors

Where Passion Meets Performance

Founded in 2008 by racing champion Derek Velocity, our agency began as a small boutique specializing in high-performance sport bikes. What started as a one-man operation in a garage has grown into a 20,000 sq ft state-of-the-art facility representing the world's finest motorcycle brands.

Our team consists of championship riders, master technicians, and industry veterans who bring decades of combined experience to every customer interaction. We're not just salespeople - we're riders who understand the thrill of the open road.

At Velocity Motors, we believe buying a motorcycle should be as exciting as riding one. That's why we've created an experience that goes beyond the typical dealership, with personalized service, exclusive events, and a commitment to the riding lifestyle that's unmatched in the industry.
