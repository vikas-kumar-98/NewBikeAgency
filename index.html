Motorcycle Purchases
What financing options do you offer?

We provide flexible financing solutions for all credit levels:

Option Terms APR
Premium Financing 12-72 months 3.99%-6.99%
First-Time Buyer 24-60 months 7.99%-12.99%
Seasonal Special 6-12 months 0% (qualifying models)

Pre-approval available online in 15 minutes without affecting your credit score.

What's included with a new motorcycle purchase?

Every new bike comes with our MotoCare Premier Package:

  • Full dealer prep and inspection
  • First service at 600 miles (free labor)
  • 2-year unlimited mileage warranty
  • 24/7 roadside assistance for first year
  • Free delivery within 100 miles

Ask about our extended warranty options at purchase.

Do you accept trade-ins?

Yes! Our trade-in process is simple:

  1. Online valuation: Get an instant estimate on our website
  2. In-person appraisal: Bring your bike for detailed inspection
  3. Trade bonus: Get extra $500 on any working motorcycle

We accept all makes/models in any condition. Even non-running bikes have value for parts.

Motorcycle Rentals
What are your rental requirements?

To rent any motorcycle, you must:

  • Be at least 21 years old (25 for premium models)
  • Have a valid motorcycle license for 2+ years
  • Provide proof of insurance or purchase our coverage
  • Leave a $500-$2000 security deposit (varies by model)

International renters need an International Driving Permit and passport.

What's included in the rental price?

All rentals include:

  • Unlimited mileage
  • Helmet (full-face or modular)
  • Basic insurance (collision damage waiver)
  • 24/7 roadside assistance
  • Lock and disc brake alarm

Optional add-ons:

  • Premium insurance ($25/day - zero deductible)
  • Passenger kit ($15/day - helmet and backrest)
  • GPS navigation ($10/day)
  • One-way rentals (available between select locations)
Can I rent a motorcycle for a cross-country trip?

Absolutely! We specialize in long-distance rentals with:

  • Weekly/monthly rates with significant discounts
  • Pre-planned touring routes with hotel partners
  • Luggage systems available (hard cases or soft bags)
  • Nationwide drop-off at partner locations

Our Coast-to-Coast Package includes 21 days unlimited mileage with emergency support along major routes.

Service & Repairs
How often should I service my motorcycle?

Recommended service intervals:

Service Type Mileage/Time Typical Cost
Basic Service Every 4,000 miles $150-$300
Major Service Every 8,000 miles $400-$800
Annual Check Every 12 months $100-$200

Severe riding conditions (dusty, rainy, or extreme temperatures) may require more frequent service.

Do you service all motorcycle brands?

Our certified technicians work on:

  • All Japanese brands (Honda, Yamaha, Kawasaki, Suzuki)
  • European models (BMW, Ducati, Triumph, KTM)
  • American motorcycles (Harley-Davidson, Indian)
  • Most vintage bikes (pre-2000 models may require special parts)

We use manufacturer-approved tools and software for all electronic diagnostics.

What's your turnaround time for repairs?

Standard repair timelines:

  • Express services: Same-day for oil changes and basic maintenance (when booked in advance)
  • Diagnostics: 1-2 business days
  • Mechanical repairs: 3-5 business days
  • Engine rebuilds: 2-3 weeks

Priority service available for an additional 25% fee (cuts wait times in half).

Safety & Training
Do you offer motorcycle training courses?

Yes! Our MotoAcademy provides:

  • Beginner Course: 2-day program with bike provided ($299)
  • Advanced Riding: Skills enhancement for experienced riders ($399)
  • Track Days: Supervised circuit training ($499/day)
  • Private Lessons: One-on-one coaching ($120/hour)

Successful completion of our beginner course waives the DMV skills test in most states.

What safety gear do you recommend?

Our ATGATT (All The Gear All The Time) policy recommends:

  • Helmet: ECE/DOT certified full-face
  • Jacket: Armored motorcycle-specific with abrasion-resistant material
  • Pants: Kevlar-lined or leather with knee protection
  • Gloves: Gauntlet style with palm sliders
  • Boots: Ankle protection and oil-resistant soles

We offer 10% discount on safety gear when purchased with any motorcycle.

Can I test ride before buying?

Test ride policies:

  • Standard models: 30-minute test rides available daily
  • Premium bikes: By appointment only (requires proof of insurance)
  • Demo days: Monthly events with full manufacturer lineups

All test riders must:

  1. Have valid motorcycle license for 3+ years
  2. Wear proper safety gear (we provide if needed)
  3. Sign liability waiver
  4. Leave a refundable $500 deposit
